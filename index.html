ACADEMIC JOURNEY

Prasadi Academy → SEE 2024 → +2 Science → SUNY Brockport, BS Nursing, Fall 2026

Building the Future of Healthcare Education in Nepal

As a student from Nepal — a country where quality healthcare education remains inaccessible to many — Sahil Kumar Karna founded NepMedGrad with a single mission: make world-class medical and scientific learning free, beautiful, and available to every child and student in Nepal.

After completing his SEE from Prasadi Academy in 2024 and his +2 with a 3.5 GPA, Sahil earned a Duolingo score of 130 and is heading to SUNY Brockport for a Bachelor of Science in Nursing, starting Fall 2026. His academic journey, built through determination and self-driven research, forms the foundation of NepMedGrad.

The platform is not a corporate product — it is a student-built scientific ecosystem. Sahil's vision is to complete his nursing education with specializations in pediatrics, oncology, and emergency care, then bring that knowledge back to Nepal through NepMedGrad's research and education infrastructure.
